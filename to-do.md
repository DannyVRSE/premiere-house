# Movie Launch company - Premiere House
a studio-adjacent house that unveils and premieres films. Think red-carpet premieres, teaser drops, and countdowns to opening night

## Task
We want a site that feels premium, cinematic, classy and state-of-the-art, while still being genuinely informative. This is the front door of a company whose entire business is making launches feel special — the site has to earn that

## What site must cover
- A signature landing experience — a hero that sells the feeling of a premiere the moment the page loads.
- Upcoming Launches — a browsable list of films with poster art, release/premiere date and a short logline, served dynamically from your Laravel API (not hard-coded in the frontend).
- Film detail — an individual page per film (synopsis, cast, date, trailer link/embed) driven by the same API.
- The company — who they are and what they do (services: premieres, campaigns, press).
- Stay in the loop — a working contact or newsletter form that is persisted through your Laravel API (stored server-side; a real success/error state).

## Stack
Next.js for the frontend (App Router or Pages Router — your choice).
Laravel / PHP for the API that serves the films and receives the form submissions.
The Next.js app consumes your Laravel API over HTTP. Use monorepo

The major focus should be the UI/UX — the feel: art direction, typography, spacing, motion, responsiveness and overall polish. A simple site that feels world-class beats a feature-packed one that feels ordinary. Make it look like something a real film company would be proud to launch on.
